Introductory Statistics

Introductory Statistics is a foundational course that covers the basic concepts and techniques of statistics. It introduces students to the principles of data collection, analysis, interpretation, and presentation. Topics typically include descriptive statistics, probability, inferential statistics, hypothesis testing, and regression analysis. This course is essential for students in various fields, including social sciences, business, and health sciences, as it provides the necessary tools to make informed decisions based on data.
